The digital space acts as a connection and positive output to the struggles that has had an impact my life.

Growing up with epilepsy as a result from a fatal injury at a young age has created barriers that I have struggled with and overcome.

Implementing whakangā acts as a space for relaxation and creative output. The works have been influenced by positive outputs of my experience with mental health and then translated into the digital space.
